About Nehaila

“Born and raised between France and Morocco!

I am a young immigrant woman who speaks 5 languages and Food is my 6th one. My mother is a Moroccan French Chef who taught me to make everything with my hands before I learned how to use machines like food processors or mixers. I learned how to “feel” my food, and understand it from raw ingredient to finished product. That is why today I am so passionate about different cuisines and cultures. “I know once people get connected to real food, they never change back.” Alice Waters - But do not forget to review it in Foodiescape.

About Hiba

A nomad at heart, with a love of food

I was born and raised in Minneapolis, MN to two immigrant parents from Somalia, escaping a deadly civil war. Early on in life, I was taught the importance of respecting other cultures and learning their stories through food. I quickly familiarized myself with middle eastern, Hispanic, Asian, and African food, and I am an avid believer that no matter where you come from or what your beliefs are, we can agree on a good dish. So we set our hearts on creating Foodiescape, for fellow food enthusiasts like ourselves to have a place to search for all of their favorite cuisines at a touch of a finger.
